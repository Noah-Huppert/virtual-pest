How I met Bill
I was just minding my own business when Bill latched himself onto my computer. I don't really want him here but I can't get him off either. So I just decided to make the best out of a bad situation and give him a nice place to live.

States
  • Happy
    • Bill is happy.
    • You can make Bill more happy by giving him some balloons
  • Thirsty
    • Bill requires liquid
    • Try giving Bill a drink to make him less thirsty
  • Hungry
    • Bill is hungry
    • Give Bill food to make him less hungry
  • Sad
    • Bill is sad, this happens a lot :(
    • But if you show Bill some balloons he won't be sad anymore
  • Tired
    • Bill is tired
    • Give Bill an energy drink to wake him up.


Behind the scenes
Behind the scenes Bill's state's are driven by Markov chains. Every second the Virtual Pest calculates its next state. Each state has a set of probabilities associated with changing to each other state (ex., if "hungry" there is a 20% chance Bill will be "thirsty" next and 80% chance Bill will stay thirsty). Each stimuli lessens the chance of a certain state occurring multiple times in a row. For example the "Tired" state: Normally there is a 50% chance that Bill will remain tired in the next state. By clicking the Energy Drink stimuli the chances of Bill being tired in the next state is much less.
